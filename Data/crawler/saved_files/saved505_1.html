archduke_piccolo (2206) on 17-Apr-16:
"You shouldn't liquidate Queens just because you are a minor piece ahead". True enough, but IN PRINCIPLE if you are a piece ahead, such a liquidation is in your interests. It all depends on how you see the game going. If your best chances are in direct attack, the the Queen may be useful there. If it looks like a positional grind, then it comes down to the relative values of the Queens in the game henceforth. If your Queen is active and the enemy's not, keep them on. But if the game is complicated and the weaker side looks to have counter-chances, then taking the Queens off the board might well be the way to simplify matters for yourself. There is a whole lot more to the issue than I've said here, but you get the picture. Whatever the situation, it is always a judgement call.
