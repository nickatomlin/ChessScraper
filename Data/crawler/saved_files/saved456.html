This was the first time in a long while that I got to play against the legendary, Jack Stockel, for a training game. That's because, as noted, we have new members. At first, Jack was training Mariann, who I've coached on 6 occasions. Of all of them, 5 were on the same year where I set record and did 11 training sessions. But, for the most part, he played against Loyd since that opponent is even stronger then me. If I had to estimate, I'd say that his rating is about 1450-1500. This means that I've been mainly paired up against Ted. However, he did not want to play against me last Friday because I have beaten him too many times. Before that, however, Jack did an equivalent to challenging me to a WTW game. That's because Jack's said that he wanted to play me with how well I've been playing against Ted. Given those circumstances combined with the fact that I don't mind losing as that's how I learned, I challenged him on Friday. Jack accepted saying that he'll do it because I wanted to play against him. I was happy. And, it was a very close game. I played extremely well here but made an inaccuracy, followed by, the decisive blunder that lost an important pawn. Afterwhich, I was busted. So, how did it all go down?
