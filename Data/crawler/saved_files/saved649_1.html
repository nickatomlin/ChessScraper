archduke_piccolo (2206) on 27-Dec-15:
I call the sort of set-up that threatens a move like this the 'masked battery'. The knight move here is the 'unmasking' of the battery. You can imagine the knight as a squadron of cavalry forming a screen behind which a company of cannon (the rook) has dropped into a battery position, ready to shoot. The cavalry moves aside, unmasking the battery's line of fire. This sort of thing could be devastating on horse and musket battlefields.
