This was a very interesting and instructive game between getoverit and Kanie. In fact, there is so much to learn that getoverit asked me to annotate this game as this was his best game. What's significant is that both sides castled short. Yet, drzemik went wrong somewhere. So, similar to Fischer's win against Byrne, getoverit sacrificed a piece for a devastating attack against white's K. That is how getoverit won this game. So, how did it go down?
