archduke_piccolo (2206) on 02-Jul-16:
This is where an 'idee fixe' can really wreck one's game. Black was thinking that, having threatened the bishop, White will now take it. But in chess, as in music, there is this concept of 'intermezzo' or 'zwischenzug', in which one plays an intervening move before reverting to the 'logical' sequence. One has always to be on the alert for that sort of thing. Black has overlooked the fact that White can play 5.Nxc6 before playing 6.cxb4. Best was probably 4...Ba5, with a certain amount of presuure against White's left-centre.
