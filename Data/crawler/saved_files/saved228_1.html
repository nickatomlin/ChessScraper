 And white resigns. Seems a little premature but black is going to take on d4 and have 5 pawns for the piece. 3 connected kingside and 2 queenside. Defending here seems hard to impossible. I find the marshall to be fun to play and think you should give it a go. Give up a pawn in the center for great attacking chances. But if white defends well early you can still give up a piece for a chain of pawns. A little technique and those pawns become queens. 5 pawns is still a ton of pawns with diminished materiel.
