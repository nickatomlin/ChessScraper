OTB Chess Game-"For Real This Time"

This was a very interesting game between me and Marriann. Now, I thought her name was Mary, so after correcting myself, I later find out that I was correct, all along. It was also a very interesting day, at the chess club. It started off with a WTW Game against Jack Stockel, but after 6 moves, he stopped it and for a good reason. It would've continued, but this runner has a new student. So, after (the other student, Mariann) made a mistake against Jack, it was over. Therefore, he had me play Mariann so that he could teach his student. And, this one didn't know anything about the rules, where you're pieces moved. I always knew that Jack is good (which is also why I can teach chess). But, he surprised me yesterday and made me smile. He taught the rules, and some of the basic openings. But, then, as Homework, he made the newbie watch games by stronger players, including but not limited to, another runner named Tom. The first one was against Mariann (with me as white,) and after she made a mistake by failing to mate me, after my mistake, he made me start a new game with Mariann. The idea being is that you don't want the new student to watch a whole bunch of mistakes. The game went like 1.e4, e5 2.Nf3, Nf6 3.Nc3, d6 4.d4, Nc6 5.dxe5, Ng4 6.exd6, Bxd6 7.Bc4, 0-0 8.0-0??, a6 9.Nd4, Bh2+ 10.Kh1 (forced), Qh4 11.Nf3, Qh5 12.Bg5, Bg3+ 13.Bh4, Nf2+ (missing the immediate win) 14.Rxf2 But, he said that best was 13...Bxh4, as now black has a mate against me, unless I accepted the loss of material. So, the game would've continued with 14.Bxh4, Qxh4+ 15.Kg1, Qh2# So, this was the new game we started which happened, at about, the same time that Tom came in. Although I played a lot better, there was not enough time to finish as the game timed out. So, it is the real deal, as I'm not making lucky blunders, this time around. Although the rest kept playing, Mariann and I had to leave at noon (especially since I had to work by 2.) In addition, since we started off with less then 40 minutes and the fact that she won against me without Jack, I wasn't going to coach, until the very end. But, seeing what Jack did with this new student (which impressed me), I'm fairly confident that she'll learn quickly with his teaching methods which is why I smiled.
