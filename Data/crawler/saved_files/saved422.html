This was my last game against Loyd on Friday. I don't mean the final one ever, but it was so for that day. Unlike the other games, we started this game without much time. As usual, it caused me to play poorly. I do play well, but I timed it as it usually happens, when there is, at least, 40 minutes as it gives me time to analyze and figure out what's going on. But, when you start off with this little bit, everything is rush. That's because I couldn't stay passed noon. First, my parents were in a little bit of a hurry, that day. Just as important is the fact that I go to work a little earlier then usual which is at 1:30. So, right afterwards, I needed to eat lunch and get ready for work. Consequently, I overlooked some very important things that the board was trying to tell me which, therefore, led to my downfall quickly. In fact, there was 10 minutes left when the game finished.
