How I overcame the "ugly Pirc"!
WP81 (1287) vs. damafi (1357)
Annotated by: damalfi (1742)
Chess opening: Pirc defence (B07)
Interactive ? Show all comments ? All annotated games ? View chessboard as:
Pages: 123Next »
As always in blitzes, I look for tactics and complications, well knowing I am a patzer, and my rating is falling because of good low-rated players... and sound mistakes by me, because of "risking too easily". Ok. Said that: this game features an UNUSUAL (for my standards) Pirc defence by me. I don't like the Pirc, it was just a slip. After a very difficult opening (as expected), I could take advantage of a not-easy-to-see (for patzers like us) potential royal pin, that allowed me to open the position to my advantage, through a complex midgame that (for my standards) is very rich in "hearthbreaking" tactic motives, that means: "it would be mate (or deadly threat) in one, BUT the opponent also has the same, so..." you know, high-voltage tactic, in which is very easy to miss a deadly "one following move", specially in a bliz.
