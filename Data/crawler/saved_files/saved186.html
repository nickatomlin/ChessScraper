As we know, next week a new tournament will start for Jack's club in which, I won't be notating OTB games for a while. But, in the meantime, this was my second game against Stanley. Now, I had a choice of white or black but picked black. The reason is because with white, it wasn't set up on the right side of the board, so it would not have been possible to annotate this game. With life getting exciting, I didn't play as well as usual. On the other hand, I still put up a fight. However, I miscalculated a move that allowed Stanley to win a piece for a pawn, with which he was going to keep his advantage. But, then, I made a mistake that ended the game at once. With that, Jack annotated a game which I thought I could bring here during an amateur championship match. But, somehow, it got corrupt which makes it impossible to annotate. The moral is how dangerous the N's can be which is what I missed there. And, after being put at a disadvantage, with that mistake, I was lost at once. So, how did it all go down?
