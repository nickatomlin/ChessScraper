This was a very interesting game against Jack Stockel. In fact, it's the first time in a couple of weeks since I defeated him. That's not too significant as it has happened before. But, it's the first time a long while that I was able to do it in a lost position. Plus, it's only the second annotation involving the win, and the first time that this win was by checkmate. That's because the first annotation involving a win was when I won his Queen. What's more is that with one miscalculation on move order, the position became corrupt which almost prevented me from being able to annotate the game. So, I spent hours trying to figure out what went down. Without a lot of time on any day due to work schedule, I had to do it over a 5 day period. But, after further move, this is exactly how the game was played. Therefore, it's the first time that I am able to do an entire annotation accurately, despite a corrupt position. Normally, I wouldn't bother, but for the OTB Campaign, and for purposes of the WTW club, I felt that it was vitle to annotate the game. That's especially because a former member named tipsyjourneyman said that I might be able to defeat my coach which is what happened with this game. The win though, wasn't just luck or oversights by him. Instead, Jack let me win for teaching purposes since I was ready to resign, after being in a lost position. So, what happened.
