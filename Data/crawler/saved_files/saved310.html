archduke_piccolo (2206) on 15-Oct-16:
In my view, this is mistimed, and ought to have waited until Black's deployment was further along. So: 7...0-0 followed by 8...Nbd7 ought to have been preferred. Now, it is true that this shuts in the c8-bishop for the moment, but later on Black can bring the King's rook to the half-open e-file, and redeploy the Queen's knight via f8 to g6 or maybe e6, with an eye to a K-side attack. Meanwhile, White will probably be interested in a 'minority attack' with pawns on the Q-side in order to create a backward or isolated Black pawn on that wing. But now we're getting into fairly arcane territory. Suffice to say that there are ways Black can make that difficult to carry through.
