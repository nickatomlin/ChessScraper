archduke_piccolo (2206) on 06-Apr-16:
Ordinarily, one playing for the draw would be the more inclined to head for the ending; who wants the win to maintain the tension. Owing to the choice of opening, Black has a slight edge in the endgame on account of White's central isolated pawn. So White doesn't want an endgame with problematical chances of survival, and would rather stay in the middle game, with a high level of tension and uncertainty as to outcome.
