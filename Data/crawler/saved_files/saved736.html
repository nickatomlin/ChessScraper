Panic? Nervousness? Black could have kept all his advantage here, by playing 12...f5! That would have been the right response to 12.Qh4, too. The point is that the knight gets immediate protection, and to win it, White would have to move her King. 13.exf6e.p. Nxf6 is useless, the knight escapes. The only convenient way is 13.Ke2.
