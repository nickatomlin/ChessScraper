What's good about this move is that your Bg2 isn't blocked by your Knight, yet. However, although it develops, 3...Nc6?! was an inaccuracy by black. Do you know why, Edna? The idea being is that it's, as you know, the QPO (Queen's Pawn Opening.) So, in 1.d4, d5 openings, you don't want to bring the Queen's Knight on the c file. Instead, this should've waited, until he advanced his c7. The idea being is that the c pawn in QPO are vital so you don't want it blocked. In doing, you already have an edge, especially since you don't make that same inaccuracy. Much better for black was 3...Nd7.
