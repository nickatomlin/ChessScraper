archduke_piccolo (2206) on 29-Jun-17:
I find it difficult to annotate Blitz games, because the time scale alters the nature of the game. How? The subjective element enters the game far more, and what can be 'best moves' in the standard form, can be second best in Blitz. This is due to the time required for the opponent to figure out its ramifications. An early Q sac for a pawn, say, is a psychological blow that White might well try in Blitz that he might well not dare in a game under standard time controls.
