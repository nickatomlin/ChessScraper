jkarp (1319) on 14-Jun-16:
Well, if Fischer said it, it must be true since it's said that he had the best analysis of anyone. In fact, Jack Stockel annotated a game with Bobby Fischer. Now, Fischer played a move, in which, everyone, including the computers thought was the losing blunder. But, the champion then found something that noonelse did. And, that is what forced a win.
