archduke_piccolo (2206) on 28-May-16:
The Exchange Variation of the Ruy Lopez. It's strategic motivation is this: though ceding Black the bishop-pair, White aims to establish an effective pawn majority on the K-side, whilst laming the Black pawn majority on the other wing. It was by this means that Em Lasker defeated J.R.Capablanca in the famous deciding game at St Petersburg, 1914. The jury is still out on this, though. Some players reckon the bishop pair is - or ought to be - the more significant factor in the game.
