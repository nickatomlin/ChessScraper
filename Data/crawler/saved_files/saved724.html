This was a very interesting game between Joe and agata. I have annotated his Q sac games, in the past, which is where he sacs his Queen early on and wins. But, they were with players whose ratings were a lot lower then his rating. Now, many players don't know what TA is trying to prove and why shows these games. On the other hand, I see the light in, just about, everything. That is especially because of how much I've learned from him. In fact, this is why despite blundering my Queen early against a student (after an oversight) I was able to force a win, regardless. And, it's also the reason why, despite being way behind in another game, I sacced my second R to force a win as there was a mating sequence. Therefore, I see the point in his Q sacs, and I have used some of Joe's methods to teach chess. In this case, however, there was not a big difference between the two ratings. On the other hand, this does not prevent Joe from saccing his Queen, and he still wins (which I am not surprised.) Given that the ratings were very close, I find this to be in need of some serious analysis. So, how did this game unfold?
