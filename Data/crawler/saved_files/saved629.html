This was another interesting game between me and the computer. It's important to note that, for a few weeks, I didn't get any coaching from Jack Stockel because he has a new student whose learning everything. The place that runs it was closed on Christmas and New Years Day. And, I will not be going tomorrow due to the cold. Afterwhich, I posted in the WTWLTR club on, "Are all the Walkers Sitting" because I was looking for a challenge. Since it didn't happen, yesterday, I decided to do an equivalent, once again through Wizard Chess as it's about Learning How to Train, or as many would call it, a different form of training game. Unlike with Fred, my goal was to last, at least, 20 moves because, on the hardest level, it's an equivalent to playing a 2400+ rated players. With easy19, it was to last 25 moves. Well, I surpassed all of that by lasting 26 moves, which is the longest I've lasted as black, on the hardest level. However, I made a few mistakes that led to my downfall. So, what happened?
