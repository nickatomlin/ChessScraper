archduke_piccolo (2206) on 27-Jan-17:
To answer your question, milly64, I am inclined to ask one: 'What do you have in mind for the b4-square'? It is too early to know yet which squares may be important, so one tends to look for: 1. Quick mobility - get your pieces, especially minor pieces out quickly; and 2. Perhaps to impose your own shape on the game by where you place your pawns. As the centre pawns are generally the more influential AND their advance usually goes towards bring the pieces into action quickly, then 1...e4, 1...e6, 1...d6 and even 1...d5 were all reasonable alternatives. 1...c5, 1...Nf6 and 1...g6 are not inconsistent with the motives of mobilization and territorial claims.
