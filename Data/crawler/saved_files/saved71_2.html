The reason why I would have considered 30...Rh8, here, is to ask White the question: what have you got? For instance, suppose White grabbed the e-file with 31.Re1. Where can it make further inroads? Black has all entry points covered, and this without his rook's help. That suggests Black can reply 31...a5, partially closing off the Q-side, and then look to counter-attack with his K-side pawns. With the rook boosting them, those pawns could become dangerously mobile. Black is still in this fight....
