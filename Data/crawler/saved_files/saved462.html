That's because one of my students faced distractions which led to some mistakes. The same thing happened here. That's because when the game ended we were, virtually, out of time. So, it was about to timeout. And, that was combined with the fact that a jazz band (that I'm familiar with and was once in) came into the Senior Center (which is where the chess club runs.) Now my area turns out to be a non sleeping joining room because, on the other side, they do activities with one being bridge. But, there are others. In this case, the jazz band was warming up. It was noisy and I felt the vibration. That combined with being almost out of time led to the super blunder that sealed my fate. So, I'm going to show my student this loss to teach her. Plus, I also mentioned about distractions in my club, so I will use this annotation as a teaching game. Therefore, how did it all go down?
