Q Sac-"Know your Principles"
tactical-abyss (2370) vs. boriskhurgin (1848)
Annotated by: jkarp (1319)
Chess opening: KP (C20), Patzer opening
Interactive ? Show all comments ? All annotated games ? View chessboard as:

This was another outstanding Blitz Queen Sac by tactical-abyss. But, there is a very important lesson to learn. Just like in my last game against, "He who must not be named", we played using the Fischer Controls with a different position. I asked how to get used to it, and he said that in order to do well you must, "Know your principles." What he meant was not to deviate from the General Opening Guidelines. Well, the same thing needs to be said with Joe's Q sacs. The idea being is that Joe will generally sac on f7 (which is the weakest pawn). So, when it's removed, especially someone of this strength, you must treat, as though, you were playing with the Fischer Controls. Plus, there's little to no room for errors. When the Queen is sacced on f7, if you start making errors, it'll often mean the end of the road. Well, in this particular game, after Joe sacced on that square, black failed to follow the General Opening Principles. And, that is what sealed his fate. So, how did it all go down?
