My plan wasn't just tempo gain but trying to win a piece by attacking something and missing the mate, which ought to not have happened. Therefore, although I told my student not to do this, it did, in another sense, become instructive. Since this is where, I, finally, understood what I did wrong against bhidragon, once and for all.
