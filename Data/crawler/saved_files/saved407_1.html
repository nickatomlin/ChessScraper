 The moral to this story is something that Jack Stockel taught me, some time ago. So, it's more then just being overly confident which is also how and why I learn from Joe's games, like this. When it appears equal, or you have an advantage then the question is does the position hold. In other words, can you keep that draw or initative. And, it's not just luck. Instead, you need to try your best and not make a mistake. Otherwise, you lose the edge and/or don't keep that draw. In general, that means that it changes from a win or draw to a loss. That's what happened in this game, as Sandre fought hard but made a mistake that overlooked this mating net. From then on, it was game over.
