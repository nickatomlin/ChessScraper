jkarp (1319) on 27-Jul-15:
I like this HW question, and this is where I feel that you explain things like a GM. There are two options. It's either 21...Nxe5 or 21...Rxe5 Both are almost the same because after black recaptures, white dare not retake, or he'd lose the Q. Personally, I think that 21...Nxe5 is best because you have more mobility. The idea being, from personal experience is that there are more outposts for the N then that of the R. In other words, the mobility would be reduced if she played ...RXB because it can't move around, as much as, the N which is why ...NXB gives it more mobility.
