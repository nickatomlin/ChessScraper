Blitz Game-Another Q Sac

This was an interesting and complex game between tactical-abyss and joseph828501. It was also very different then other Q sac's that I've seen. In fact, I got the moral a little wrong, last time, being that, even with a Q down, when the f7 pawn is removed, you must be very careful. You must look out for things similar to Fools Mate or Scholar's Mate which happens right on the f7 square since that is the weak spot. So, if you're not careful, and play with very little to no error, it will often spell trouble. But, this time, TA sacs his Q on h7, which changes the dynamic. Now, game between Karpov and Susan Polgar, I decided to study this game and annotate it as homework. And, given where white sac's his Q and how the long the game lasts, this time, there is theory affiliated with it.
