 Consistent with the idea of playing a Colle, but rather a conservative line. I half expected White to play 3.Bg5 - the Veresov Attack. You might notice that the thing would have looked like a left-handed Ruy Lopez: quite playable. Otherwise, White might have played a quiet developing line like 3.Nf3, or even something wild and woolly: 3.e4!?, the dreaded Blackmar-Diemer Gambit (3.e4 dxe4 4.f3 exf3 5.Nxf3 ... with quick development for White). Developing players wishing to hone their attacking skills could do worse than try out the Blackmar-Diemer.
