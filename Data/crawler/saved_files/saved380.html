3....f6 is a very weak move. I can see that you were perhaps scared of your opponent playing Ng5, but that is nothing to worry about, as it can always be met with e6. The problem with 3....f6 is that it doesn't help your development, and weakens your king position. 3...e6, 3...d6 3...g6 3....Nf6 or 3... a6 are all better moves.
