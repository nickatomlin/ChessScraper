A glimpse into the mind of Tipsy!

As I play, I annotate. This is the opposite, I believe, of what everyone else does, annotating once finished but, well, I find annotating in situ improves my chess and appreciation of the ebb and flow of the game. Afterwards, I clean up the annotation for public consumption. BUT, this time, I thought I'd give readers a glimpse into the disturbed and drunken mind of the Tipsy by leaving these two annotations as is. Of course that means retaining the emotion of the moment, so apologies if there's anything in here that's over the top or offensive!
