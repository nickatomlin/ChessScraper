A bit of a roller-coaster ride - the game could have gone either way. The thing to watch for are short range captures and tactics. Always look around for what can capture what. Do it quite cosciously - even develop a routine of looking at each piece and pawn in turn, to see what it can do. This will help reduce leaving things 'en prise' or overlooking similar mistakes by your opponent.
