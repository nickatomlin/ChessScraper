The first time that I've coached someone, in a while. I've been doing it on and off a lot, lately. That was, especially after that unlucky year where I became well known for doing this, despite it involving a certain someone. Later it stopped. Then, after everything settled down, I did even more. And, in 2015, I went from being inactive (for a long while) to setting a record, in terms of how many training games I did in that year. Totals were 11, and the most amount of training games I did before was a year prior, but it topped 6. Much later on, it stopped but not because I was told not to do that. Instead, I wound up watching the last season of American Idol. Be it note that these singing competitions have also helped my play because you look back in time, and the contestants must go through a lot and at difficult levels, to win the competition. And, when reflecting on it, I realize that some of the things that they must do is what I must cope with to become a better player, like getting out of my comfort zone and raising the bar so that each Homework gets more difficult. With that being said, since Jack Stockel wanted to play Loyd and Ted played Perry, it worked out perfectly that I got to play Marriann. Since we were there, despite the loss last time, I decided to take time to coach her since my attitude is still helping players improve which is why you see me comment Edna's annotation, on a regular basis. In addition, Mr. Stockel was tied down due to his game with Loyd, and he went to look at how Ted and Perry were playing. That it a one to one coaching session (which I enjoy). This time, it was, a, "No Mr. nice guy." And, I won in doing so. What this shows is that I can and do teach chess, even today. So, how did this game unfold?

Black might as well have played 4...fxe5. Unlike the Damiano Defence proper, 5.dxe5 fxe5!? 6.Nxe5? simply loses a piece for two pawns, as after 6...dxe6 7.Qh5ch Kd7! 8.Qxe5 Qf6, White has no more useful attack.  The real reason for playing this, as opposed to 5.exf6 was to eliminate the doubled pawns. I wasn't sure, if they were a liablility here.
