This was an interesting blitz game between reversion and his opponent. It's important to note that this particular annotation is based on his chess puzzle that I solved and then commented. From there, reversion let us know how he was able to mate. In fact, I will be copying and pasting some of his variations and quote what he said because I think this is good for learning purposes, as well. In this particular game, black went wrong right in the opening with Hope Chess. He was attempting to set up a trap, but against a stronger player it doesn't work. So, attempting is what led to black's downfall.
