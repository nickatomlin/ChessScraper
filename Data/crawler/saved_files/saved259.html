archduke_piccolo (2206) on 04-Dec-16:
'Thematic' - not an easy word to explain. You may have found in your games there develops a 'theme' which might be attack on the King, or pressure on a weak point, expansion on the wing, or in the centre. These are long-term ideas that might emerge as the game proceeds. In this game, White still has the makings of a big centre, not realised (made real) just yet, but the potential is there. So Black will be interested in undermining or attacking that centre, if possible even before it gets fully built up and dangerous.. So the move is 'thematic' in the sense that it goes towards that idea - that 'theme'. If White were now to play 10.e4!?, that too would be 'thematic' in the sense of his ambition to build his centre. Would it be good here? Premature perhaps? A judgement call: it seems to me playable enough. In this game White plays more circumspectly. He would like to play e4 soon, make no doubt about that!
