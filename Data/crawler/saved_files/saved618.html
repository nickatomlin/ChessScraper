This is a well-known motif, and you will notice that after 16.Qxg6ch, even if White had absolutely no further attacking resources, he'd have a forced draw. This can be a game-saving motif! But suppose Black let the Bishop live? The only reasonable defence seems to be 15...Qe7, when things can get very interesting: 16.Ne4! ...
