 The infamous Latvian Gambit. This opening is denigrated by chess masters, and shunned by just about every GM you can think of. But for those adventurous (and crazy) enough to play it, it can be a lethal weapon unlike no other opening for black. It is an opening for attackers, for the tactically-minded and fearless who would rather go down with explosions and fire than fizzle-out positionally in a quiet endgame.
