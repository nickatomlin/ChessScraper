archduke_piccolo (2206) on 19-May-16:
GM Bent Larsen once (more than once, probably) expressed his doubts about the Open Sicilan, on the grounds that White, all of his own accord, gives up a centre pawn for a flank one (actually, I tend to think of the c- and f-files as semi-flank, but that's by the by). It seems to me that 7.Nxc6 rather aggravates the problem. Mitigated by the backward pawn, White could tolerate Black's majority, but now that the d-pawn is not so backward, and the d5-square no longer a weakness for Black, White has made a very serious concession.
