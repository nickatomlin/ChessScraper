jkarp (1319) on 24-Sep-15:
A very good swindle. You were lost, but then found a way out and turned it into a draw. I agree that you were right to stay in, and that's why Jack always says, "Don't give up right away", which I have emphasized to my students. That's because, as he's told me, "Anything can happen." You never after being lost, if your opponent will blunder and allow you to pull a swindle like this. And, if you resign prematurely, you just won't find out. Whereas, it's better to stay in because, even in a lost position, if the opponent goes wrong, somewhere, that loss can turn, as it did here. This was, therefore, a very instructive game.
