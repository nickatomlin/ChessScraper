archduke_piccolo (2206) on 30-Jan-17:
The exchange sacrifice (...cxb5) used to be considered quite playable for Black - even for a time a refutation of the whole 8.Qf3 line. Theory has changed, of course. Yakov Estrin considered 8...cxb5 not quite sufficient 'if White continues accurately' (Ya. Estrin 'The Two Knights Defence c.1970). The very first time I has Black in this line, and not having seen it before, I tried the exchange sac. I lost the game, but felt that it was not the fault of the variation.
