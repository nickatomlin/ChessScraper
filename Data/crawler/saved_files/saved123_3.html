archduke_piccolo (2206) on 07-Apr-17:
In my view 57.Re3 is ... well, possibly not a mistake exactly, but an inaccuracy. It won't lose, but it is a good plan (as a rule) to maintain what is called 'checking distance'. That enables the defender to check along a rank whilst making sure that the King, tied to defending a pawn, can not come after the rook. I would have played 57.Ra3, making REALLY sure. Also good, as it happens, would have been 57.Rc4, forming a barrier to Black's advance.
