Losing Battle, Won War
teardrop34 (2170) vs. tombulous (1869)
Annotated by: teardrop34 (2191)
Chess opening: Ruy Lopez (C85), Exchange variation doubly deferred (DERLD)
Interactive ? Show all comments ? All annotated games ? View chessboard as:
This game exemplifies 2 classic ideas I like about this game: anyone can be caught off-guard at any time. I nearly fell victim to this. And...2: if you fail to keep the initiative and go passive for even a moment, just be wary about the threats about your opponent is posing on you. Here in this game, I literally was in a bad position for over 80% of the game, but due to a late game blunder, I was able to turn everything around and make a competitive run at the end. Let's look at the specifics shall we?
