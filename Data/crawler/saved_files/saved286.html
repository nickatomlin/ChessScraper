"Carlson-Karjakin World Champion 2016 round 01"
GM Carlsen vs. GM Karjakin
Annotated by: jkarp (1319)
Chess opening: Trompovsky attack (Ruth, Opovcensky opening) (A45)

This was a very interesting game between some of the world's best players in the world. In fact, Carlson is competing to remain the world champion, and Karjakin is competing for that title. So, the title of this annotation is what the game was called which is why it's quoted. So, not surprisingly, this game was annotated by a GM. His name is Alexander Delchev. And, it is the first time in a long while that I've had luck finding a game annotated by a GM. As I have been copying and pasting these annotations onto this site by googling them from other sites. But, there were only a few. My luck, however, changed because alex_ratchkov posted a link from the WTWLTR club which includes this annotation. The annotators take on this game is very interesting, there are a lot of variations that I'm excited to play through.
