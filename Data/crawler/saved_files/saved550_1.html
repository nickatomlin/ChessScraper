elyhim (2241) on 09-Mar-16:
In the middle game phase when there are a lot of pieces on the board it is generally better to keep the king in the center. However, toward the endgame when there are a few pieces on the board the king can become a very powerful piece. King+pawn endings are won by the one who can get their king over the center line first.
