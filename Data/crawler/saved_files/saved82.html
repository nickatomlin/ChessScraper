Time was the key factor, but still the game presents 1) two very exposed kings - none castled. 2) Black kept two pieces in their initial squares until the very end of the game. Incredibly, the only active role of one of this piece was the key factor in black's win. Enjoy, correct if you feel, and take it as it is, a fun blitz, nothing more.
