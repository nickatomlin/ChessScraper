Interactive ? Show all comments ? All annotated games ? View chessboard as:

In it he said something that Kenneth_fiore_3 once told. Don't wait until mate. When you're faced with ruinous loss of material, that's when it's time to resign, as you'll learn nothing by continuing the game. And, the idea behind this tourney was to learn. But, I blundered early and paid the price. So, how did it all go down?
