I have, as Homework, studied a lot of Joe's Queen sacs since Jack has given good advice of not resigning so early, even when, I thought I was lost. The only time he ever said it was ok was when you're a Q down or far behind and can't compensate which a lot do agree that it would be resignable position. But, Joe gave different advice of not resigning because you just don't know when you're opponent will go wrong. His Q sacs are perfect examples because he gives up his Q for a pawn early and wins. Lately, I have been studying them, with Joe playing as white. However, due to my good annotations, I met another player (reversion). And, he asked me to annotate other games. But, it was where black that is also in the top 100 rated players on this website (or top 100 rated GK players), play dubious defenses and wins from their positional skills. He showed me a game (which I intend to annotate the at some point.) This is another good example because Joe is black and he sacs his Q early on, but not to my surprise, he wins.
