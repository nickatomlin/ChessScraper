This was an interesting game between me and mickymaus. But, this is where, figuring what's unfolding and how to respond (how the game's developing) becomes important. As usual, I failed to do that, in this particular game. For one thing, I was hyperfocused on developing and castling K side, as opposed to, castling long, right away. But, then, there were other factors. Failing to understand the entire story is what led my downfall here.

 Wanting to learn by trying something different, I play the Philidor's Defense. I like to try different things that I haven't done before since for me, I'm used to responding after the Ruy Lopez.
