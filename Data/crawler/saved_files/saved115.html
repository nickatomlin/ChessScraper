This was an interesting game between Tom Walsh and me. The first game was a tournament, and I did ok until the end game. In fact, I was a piece up. But, after he got back a Rook, I thought that it was a mate, checked, and overlooked that his Queen was on the same diagnol as mine. And, yes, we both played the first game way too fast. As we both started off with 45 minutes, but ended with him having 30 min and me having 25 min. Jack pointed out that we should be at the last few minutes as that's why the GM's have so much time in the tournament. The point is that you can't play a tournament game as fast as a regular OTB game. After losing, he promoted a pawn. I thought that there was a chance, so I kept pushing my passed pawn. But, I was wrong, as he checked with his Queen and had the Rook behind there from which, Tom would win my passer. So, I resigned there. In this game, I was taken off guard and missed what happened in the opening, as I got it confused with the Spanish Opening. That resulted in me losing a pawn early. But, then I made a terrible mistake that lost at once. So, how did it all go down?
