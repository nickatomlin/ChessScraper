jkarp (1319) on 21-Nov-16:
If it were up to me, I'd rather lose the exchange because losing even one pawn, could spell a disaster in the endgame. That's why Jack Stockel says, "Don't give things away." By that, he's also alluding to pawns. And, that fact is so important that I've wound up teaching this to some of my students because it boils down to what an extra pawn can do in the endgame.
