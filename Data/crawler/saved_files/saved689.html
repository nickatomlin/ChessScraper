Believe it or not, 7.Qxg7?! was an inaccuracy. Capturing here is known to be dangerous. Best was, thus, 7...Rg8 You wouldn't be able to castle K side, you would've gained tempo. The idea being is that he can't take on f6 due to your Queen on d8. And, he can't take on h7, due to your Nf6. That would leave you in much better shape, despite any recompensation. With that being said, 7...d5?? is a super blunder because of white's next move, which will also prevent you from castling.
