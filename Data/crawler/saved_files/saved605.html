Benoni mentoring game #1: I recently am playing Queens pawn games with master players. I am interested in defenses that can defeat the queens pawn, as I struggle with this opening when playing black. I have come to understand Elyhim as an expert at the Benoni system (demostrated here) and therefore, having access to him through WTW club, asked if he would mentor me in a series of games. This is the first. I sincerely thank him for the time he spent discussing the various options, strengths and potential pitfalls during the game. This is quite a long game at 49 moves, so I will try to abbreviate the annotation to the "eventful" moments from my perspective. I am playing white and Elyhim is black.
